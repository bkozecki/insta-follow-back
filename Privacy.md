# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2025**

## The short version

Folo does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.
Everything happens in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

## The full version

### What data we process

Folo processes the JSON files you upload (followers.json and following.json)
to compare your Instagram followers and following lists.

### Where data is processed

All processing happens **entirely in your browser** using client-side
JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to any server. No data is sent
to us or any third party at any point.

### What we store

Nothing. Folo does not use cookies, local storage, session storage, or
any other persistent storage mechanism. When you close or refresh the page,
all data is gone.

### Analytics and tracking

Folo does not use any analytics tools, tracking scripts, advertising pixels,
or third-party services that collect user data.

### Third-party services

Folo is hosted on Cloudflare Pages / Vercel. Their infrastructure may log
basic request metadata (IP address, timestamp) as part of standard CDN
operation. This is outside our control and governed by their respective
privacy policies.

### Your rights under GDPR

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no data to
access, rectify, or delete.

### Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update the "last updated"
date above and note the change in the project changelog.
